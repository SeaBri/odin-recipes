Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Can of Crushed Tomato
  • Two Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • Two Cans of Tomato Paste
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Dried Basil
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Egg

Directions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna in the following order:
    • Meat sauce
    • Noodles
    • Ricotta mixture
    • Mozzarella
    • Meat sauce
    • Parmesan
    • Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes at 375F
  6. After 25 minutes bake uncovered for 25 more minutes at 375F
  7. Let lasagna rest for 15 minutes
