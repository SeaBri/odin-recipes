Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients:

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts cut into cubes
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Celery Seed
  • Chicken Broth
  • Milk
  • Pie Crust

Directions

  1. Boil the cubed chicken with the carrots, peas, and celery for 15 minutes
  2. Drain and set aside
  3. cook the onions in butter until they are translucent
  4. Stir in the flour and seasonings
  5. Add the chicken broth and milk, simmering until the filling is thick
  6. Place the filling in your pie crust
  7. Cover the top crust, seal the edges, and cut slits in the top to allow steam to escape
  8. Bake until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling
